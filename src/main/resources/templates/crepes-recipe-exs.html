• Flour (125g)
  • Eggs (2)
  • Milk (240ml)
  • Water (120ml)
  • Butter (2 tablespoons)
  • Sugar (1 tablespoons)
  • Salt
  • Berry sauce or jam

How to cook

In a bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar (optional), and salt. Add the eggs, then gradually whisk in the milk and water until the butter is smooth. Stir in the melted butter (or oil), and let the batter rest briefly. Heat a lightly greased nonstick skillet over medium heat. Pour about Â¼ cup of batter into the pan, swirl it around to coat, and cook until the bottom is golden. Flip, cook the other side for a short time, then stack the finished crepes to keep them warm. Spread each crepe with berry sauce or jam, add whipped cream or yogurt, fold, and (if you like) sprinkle with powdered sugar or crushed cookies/nuts. Serve warm.
