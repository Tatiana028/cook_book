• Bread (4 slices)
  • Milk (100 ml)
  • Egg (1)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil

How to cook

In a deep bowl, whisk the egg with the milk, then add salt and pepper. Dip the bread slices into the mixture. Fry them on both sides in a hot skillet until golden brown. Serve with sauce or any toppings you like.
